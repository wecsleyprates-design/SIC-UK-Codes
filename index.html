The Strategic Model (Appendix J)

Q1: Exactly what happens in the flow and with the sources?

The flow is a Four-Stage Convergence: Ingestion (Hunting for prefixes), Extraction (Filtering by 'gb_sic'), Resolution (Weighting OC at 0.9 vs AI at 0.1), and Persistence (Writing to the new DB column).

The Prompt Logic: Our AI Enrichment prompt is now "Country-Aware." It receives the country fact and switches its mental model to the local standard (e.g., UK SIC 2007 for GB).

Q2: How we handle the new SIC UK within the current flow?

We insert a Parallel Fact Junction. Instead of overhauling NAICS, we add uk_sic_code as a first-class citizen fact. If registry data is missing, the AI triggers automatically to fill the gap before scoring.

Q3: Have this as a model for other countries?

This is the Worth International SIC Model. Scaling AU, DE, or EU now only takes two lines of code: (1) Register the prefix (e.g., au_anzsic) and (2) Set the validator. The AI engine handles the thinking for every jurisdiction automatically.
