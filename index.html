18. Troubleshooting FAQ

Why not just map UK SIC to NAICS?

Crosswalks are inaccurate. Extracting native codes directly ensures compliance with regional standards without translation loss.

Registry vs AI Disagreement?

Registry (Weight 0.9) wins unless marked dormant. AI (Weight 0.1) fills gaps or overrides stale 1992 codes.
