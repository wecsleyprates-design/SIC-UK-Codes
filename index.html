UK SIC Classification Strategy

Transforming worth-wide industrial classification for GB jurisdictions.

The Core Problem

Our current system is US-centric. While we capture UK SIC codes from registry data, our pipeline silently discards it in favor of NAICS codes that often have no relevance to the UK market.

Mapping the Data Loss

This is exactly why your current UK business reports are missing SIC data:

1
OpenCorporates Ingestion

Raw string received: gb_sic-62020|us_naics-541511

2
The Filtering Bottleneck

Logic in lib/facts/businessDetails looks specifically for "naics". It extracts the US code and drops the 62020 code entirely.

3
Vendor Pollution

Trulioo returns US SIC 7372. Without a validated UK-specific fact, this polluted data overwrites or conflicts with real registry values.
