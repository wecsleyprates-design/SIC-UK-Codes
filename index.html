The Data Loss Analysis

Our audit of 2,344 managed UK businesses revealed that industry data is present at the source but systematically discarded due to Architectural Siloing. We have mapped the "Data Loss" in three critical failure points.

1
Manual Extraction Conflict: The system ingested gb_sic-62020|us_naics-541511 but the filter in integration-service pulled ONLY the US code, discarding the authoritative UK data.
2
Vendor Conflict: Vendors like Trulioo contribute 4-digit SIC codes from the US 1972 standard, polluting the UK business profile with US-centric categories.
3
Persistence Conflict: The data_businesses schema lacks a uk_sic_code junction, causing any correctly resolved data to "fall through" the system.
