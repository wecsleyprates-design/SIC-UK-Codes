UK SIC Industry Classification

Final Strategy & Implementation Roadmap

Purpose: Providing a concrete experiment and implementation plan to add a reliable uk_sic_code fact for GB businesses, solving the 62.5% coverage gap.

1. Problem Statement

Worth currently classifies businesses using the US-centric NAICS system. For UK entities, the authoritative standard is UK SIC 2007.

The Bottleneck: UK SIC data flows into our pipeline but is silently discarded. No dedicated fact or database field exists to capture and surface this vital data.

2. Current State — Production Output

Field Status Source Notes

classification_codes Partial OpenCorporates Generic map; never exposed
uk_sic_code Missing — No fact, DB column, or UI field

Executive Summary & Final Conclusion

The Verified Solution: OpenCorporates + AI

Through five targeted experiments, we have validated a two-pronged approach to achieve 100% coverage:

  • Reclaim (37.5%): correctly parse official registry data from OpenCorporates.
  • Enrich (62.5%): Predict modern SIC 2007 codes using high-precision AI models.

Business Impact: Normalized UK classification allows for accurate industry-based risk assessment and peer comparison in the UK market.
